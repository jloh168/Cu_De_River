06 — Risk Assessment

Know the risks

We present risks transparently. US investors in particular should review FCPA compliance before committing capital.

Critical

FCPA / OFAC Compliance

The 10% profit allocation to "ABC Company (government partner)" raises significant flags under the US Foreign Corrupt Practices Act. US investors face potential criminal liability regardless of Vietnamese legality.

Independent FCPA compliance review of all government-related payments required before US investor commitment.
High

Foreign Ownership Restrictions

Under Vietnam's 2024 Land Law, foreigners cannot directly own land. The Red Book is held by the local Vietnamese entity. If the nominee structure fails, US investor legal recourse is limited.

Engage reputable Vietnamese law firm. Structure with strong contractual protections and international arbitration clause.
High

Permitting Uncertainty

The entire revenue model depends on mining/extraction permits within 3–5 months. Permits can be delayed or denied due to government policy shifts, new leadership, or environmental concerns. The $2M down payment is committed with no revenue if permits are denied.

Seek conditional permit approvals before committing full capital. Build government relationships early.
Medium-High

Capital Repatriation & Currency Risk

Profit repatriation from Vietnam requires audited financials, tax clearance, and State Bank compliance. VND has historically depreciated 3–5% annually vs USD, eroding returns for dollar-denominated investors.

Pre-structure repatriation plan with State Bank approval. Consider Singapore SPV for cleaner currency flows.
Medium-High

Legal Enforcement

Vietnam's legal system does not provide protections US investors are accustomed to. "LP priority return" has no statutory backing in Vietnamese corporate law. Contract enforcement is inconsistent.

Include international arbitration clause. Use escrow arrangements for critical milestone payments.
Medium

Soil Market Risk

If infrastructure projects slow or soil prices fall below $1.50/m³, revenue projections are impacted. The conservative $1.50 price (vs $1.50–$2.50 market) provides some buffer.

Secure long-term supply agreements with contractors. 3-vendor model diversifies buyer concentration risk.
07 — Legal & Compliance

Structuring for compliance

Proper legal structuring protects both your investment and your ability to repatriate profits. The recommended Singapore SPV structure maximizes compliance and capital mobility.

Required Permits (Vietnam)

  • Mining License — Ministry of Natural Resources & Environment
  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
  • Da Nang People's Committee approval
  • Red Book (Land Use Rights Certificate) — prerequisite
  • Business License & Tax Registration
  • Transport Permit for soil haulage

Foreign Investment Accounts

  • DICA — Direct Investment Capital Account. For companies with >51% foreign ownership. Only DICA-routed capital can be legally repatriated.
  • IICA — Indirect Investment Capital Account. For 49% foreign / 51% Vietnamese structure. Governed by Circular 03/2025/TT-NHNN (eff. June 2025).
  • SWIFT purpose field must state: "Investment Capital into [Company Name]"
  • Remitting entity must match IRC shareholder name exactly.
⭐ Recommended Structure

Singapore SPV — Establish a Special Purpose Vehicle in Singapore as the holding entity. Singapore has a favorable tax treaty with Vietnam, significantly easier capital flows in and out, and serves as a clean intermediary for Chinese, US, or other foreign investors routing capital into Vietnam. The Vietnamese project company (49% foreign / 51% Vietnamese) receives capital via IICA from the Singapore entity.

Chinese Investor ODI Path

  • NDRC filing → Outbound Investment Project Registration Certificate
  • MOFCOM approval → Enterprise Overseas Investment Certificate
  • SAFE registration → Foreign exchange registration at bank
  • Practical alternative: Route via Hong Kong or Singapore holding company
  • Never use underground exchanges — funds cannot be legally repatriated if not bank-routed
