# PART 1: PROJECT OVERVIEW

## Strategic Advantages
- Prime location near Da Nang International Airport
- Da Nang is Vietnam's 3rd largest airport hub, undergoing major expansion
- Key corridor between Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City — massive highway construction demand
- Lower land cost than HCMC/Hanoi with high appreciation potential

## Core Revenue Model: Soil Extraction & Monetization
Vietnam is in a golden era of infrastructure buildout. Highway projects create enormous demand for fill soil. Once land title (Red Book) is transferred, soil sales can begin immediately.

- **Buyer-operated model**: Buyers handle all excavation, machinery, and transport — minimizing our operational cost and risk
- **On-site sales**: We sell at the mine gate; buyers take full operational responsibility
- **Revenue timing**: Cash flow expected within 5 months of title transfer

## Two-Phase Profit Model

### Phase 1: Soil Extraction (Years 1–5)
- Total reserves: ~5,000,000 cubic meters
- Annual target: 1,000,000 cubic meters/year
- Price: ~$1.50/m³ (conservative) → $6,840,000/year revenue
- Soil sales revenue over 5 years projected to exceed $10,000,000

### Phase 2: Land Sale (Year 5+)
- After extraction, land is fully leveled — ready for development
- Target buyers: large real estate developers (eco-tourism, commercial, residential)
- Conservative residual land value: **$55,000,000**

# PART 5: LEGAL & FUND TRANSFER COMPLIANCE

## Vietnam: Foreign Investment Account Structure
- **DICA** (Direct Investment Capital Account): For companies with >51% foreign ownership or holding an IRC. Required channel for foreign capital; only DICA-routed funds can be legally repatriated as profit.
- **IICA** (Indirect Investment Capital Account): For companies with ≤50% foreign ownership (e.g., 49/51 structure). Governed by Circular 03/2025/TT-NHNN (effective June 2025).

## China: Outbound Direct Investment (ODI) Path
**Formal route:**
1. NDRC filing → Outbound Investment Project Registration Certificate
2. MOFCOM approval → Enterprise Overseas Investment Certificate
3. SAFE registration → Foreign exchange registration at bank

**Practical route:** Use Hong Kong or Singapore holding company to remit to Vietnam DICA/IICA. Remitting entity must match shareholder name on Vietnam IRC.

## Recommended Structure for This Project
- 49% foreign / 51% Vietnamese → open IICA for foreign shareholder
- SWIFT message purpose field: "Investment Capital into [Company Name]"
- **Recommended**: Establish Singapore SPV — favorable tax treaty with Vietnam, easier capital flows

## Absolute Prohibitions
- No underground money exchanges or informal transfers
- Funds not routed through banking system cannot be recognized as legal investment
- Non-compliant capital cannot be repatriated as profit

# PART 7: LICENSING REQUIREMENTS

Required permits for soil extraction in Vietnam:
1. **Mining License** — Issued by Ministry of Natural Resources & Environment
2. **Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)** — Compliance with Vietnamese environmental standards
3. **Local Government Approval** — Da Nang People's Committee
4. **Red Book** — Land Use Rights Certificate (prerequisite)
5. **Business License & Tax Registration** — Vietnamese company registration
6. **Transport Permit** — May be required for soil haulage
